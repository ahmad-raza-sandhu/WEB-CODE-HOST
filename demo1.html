Technical Requirements

  • Certified electric & steam setup by qualified technician
  • Proper ventilation and clearance for steam dissipation
  • Reliable power supply for electric function (standard 110V-240V)
  • Indoor placement only - not suitable for exposed outdoor locations
  • Clean water supply for steam function (distilled recommended)
  • Level, stable surface with proper weight distribution

  • Flame controlled and safe for pets and children
  • Auto-shutoff when water level is low (steam function)
